Week 1 Recap & Week 2 Kick-Off · April 27–29, 2026

Hi Partner Advisory Board (PAB),

That's a wrap on Week 1 of our Spring 2026 event! Three half-day sessions shared exclusively with this partner community, covering a packed lineup of senior leaders and product engineering teams from across the Dynamics 365 ecosystem. Thank you to everyone who joined live and shared candid feedback in the polls and chat.

This is your advisory board — your opportunity to be heard. We welcome and encourage everyone to share perspectives, ask questions, and provide feedback throughout. These insights play a key role in influencing our strategic direction, and ensuring our joint success.

Key themes from Week 1

  • Agentic AI is an enterprise shift — redefining how customers operate by moving from human-led processes to autonomous, AI-driven decision-making and orchestration across the business.
  • Partner operating model evolution — moving into high-value services spanning strategic advisory, scalable agent development, enablement and adoption, and managed services for AI solutions.
  • CRM and ERP as the intelligence layer — pre-built first-party agents now span Sales, Service, Contact Center, Finance & HR, Supply Chain, Project Ops, and Field Service, with partner extensibility via Copilot Studio and MCP.
  • Intelligence + trust as the foundation — Copilot to bring intelligence into every task, agents to drive autonomous task execution, and Agent 365 as the governed control plane underpinning it all.
  • Governance, security, and Responsible AI front-and-center — Entra agent IDs, Purview sensitivity labels, Defender prompt-injection protections, and a single admin plane.
